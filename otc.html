OpenC2 is a standardized language for the command and control of technologies that provide or support cyber defenses.

The specifications and the standards for OpenC2 are developed in the OASIS Open Command and Control (OpenC2) Technical Committee (TC).

The OpenC2 community consists of cybersecurity stakeholders across government agencies, small to large industries across all sectors, and academia.
