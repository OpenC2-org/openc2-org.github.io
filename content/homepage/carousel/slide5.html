April 2024 Cybersecurity Automation Village

OpenC2 TC member organizations will be participating in the 11-12 April 2024 Cybersecurity Automation Village. The Village, sponsored by the Open Cybersecurity Alliance CASP subproject, is for sharing information, prototyping, testing, and specifying interoperability among cybersecurity automation technologies. OpenC2 will participate in use cases involving the Kestrel Threat Hunting Language and CACAO-based automation.
