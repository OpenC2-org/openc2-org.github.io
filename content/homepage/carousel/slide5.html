April 2025 Cybersecurity Automation Village

OpenC2 TC member organizations are participating in the 2-3 April 2024 Cybersecurity Automation Village. The Village, sponsored by the Open Cybersecurity Alliance CASP subproject, addresses sharing information, prototyping, testing, and specifying interoperability among cybersecurity automation technologies.
