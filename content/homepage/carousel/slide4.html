Information Modeling with JADN Committee Note published!

The OpenC2 TC has approved Information Modeling with JADN v1.0 as an OASIS Committee Note. This CN describes the use of Information Models, explains how to construct IMs using JADN, and contrasts IMs with other modeling approaches, such as Entity-Relationship models for databases, and knowledge models / ontologies.
