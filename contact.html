OpenC2 Members

OpenC2 TC membership is required to send to any of these lists.

OpenC2Technical Committee Discussion List

TC members can post to the OpenC2 TC discussion list.



Non Members and General Information

All Members and non-members can request information using these lists.

Post comments about OpenC2 TC technical work

Non-members can contribute comments on OpenC2 TC work products using the openc2-comment discussion list (creation of an OASIS community platform account is required).

Join OASIS or Contact Member Services

For information about participation in OASIS visit their Get Involved page.


Both the TC and comment discussion lists are publicly viewable and include all content since the founding of the OpenC2 TC in June 2017.
