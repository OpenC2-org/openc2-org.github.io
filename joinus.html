Cyber security professionals involved in cyber-defense technology development and integration are encouraged to use OpenC2 technical artifacts, language specifications and actuator profiles.

OpenC2 technical artifacts are housed in GitHub.

You can also get involved in the OASIS OpenC2 technical committee to contribute to the on-going development and evolution of the standards.

The following subcommittees are active in the OpenC2 TC:

  • OpenC2 Actuator Profile Subcommittee - Defining actuator profiles, the OpenC2 message elements applicable to specific cyber defense functions.
  • OpenC2 Implementation Considerations Subcommittee - Providing guidance for implementation aspects such as message transport and information assurance.
  • OpenC2 Language Subcommittee - Developing, maintaining, and resolving comments to the OpenC2 language documentation.
